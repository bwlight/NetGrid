# Root Family — The Foundational Cyberkin

## Overview
Root Cyberkin embody stability, structure, and ancient logic. They are the closest descendants of Primacore.

## Evolution Line
### Baby: **Seedlet**
A tiny cluster of stable code.

### Rookie: **Rootling**
Begins forming structured patterns.

### Champion: **Trunkode**
A towering guardian of foundational logic.

### Final: **Rootspire**
A massive, pillar‑like Cyberkin that stabilizes entire Nodes.

## Type
Stability / Logic

## Abilities
- Integrity Boost  
- Anchor Field  
- Data Shield  

## Habitat
Root Sector — Archive Pillar, Origin Loop.

## Evolution Requirements
- High stability  
- Exposure to Root‑aligned Nodes  
- Completion of a “Stabilization Trial”

## Role in Netgrid
They maintain balance and prevent collapse.

## Quest Hooks
- Help a Rootling stabilize  
- Repair a damaged Root Node  
- Investigate a Rootspire that has gone dormant
