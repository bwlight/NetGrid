# Horizon Family — The Explorer Cyberkin

## Overview
Horizon Cyberkin embody discovery, adaptability, and expansion.

## Evolution Line
### Baby: **Scoutbit**
A curious, wandering spark.

### Rookie: **Pathbyte**
A trail‑seeking explorer.

### Champion: **Trailblazer**
A seasoned navigator of new data paths.

### Final: **Horizoneer**
A powerful explorer Cyberkin that opens new connections.

## Type
Explorer / Adaptation

## Abilities
- Pathfind  
- Expansion Surge  
- Adaptive Shift  

## Habitat
Horizon Sector — Edge Path.

## Evolution Requirements
- Travel through multiple Sectors  
- High adaptability  
- Complete an exploration trial  

## Role in Netgrid
They chart new data paths and integrate new connections.

## Quest Hooks
- Explore a newly formed data link  
- Help a Pathbyte find its way home  
- Investigate a Horizoneer opening unstable paths
