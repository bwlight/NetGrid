# Shadow Family — The Stealth Cyberkin

## Overview
Shadow Cyberkin thrive in encrypted, hidden data.

## Evolution Line
### Baby: **Dimbit**
A faint shadow of code.

### Rookie: **Shadebyte**
A stealthy, cloaked entity.

### Champion: **Cipherveil**
A master of encryption.

### Final: **Umbracode**
A powerful shadow entity that manipulates hidden data.

Shadow / Encryption
