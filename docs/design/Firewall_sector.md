Firewall Sector
🔥 SECTOR 02 — FIREWALL
“The Protective Flame”
Primal Sector — One of the First Three

⭐ I. Sector Overview
Firewall is the Grid’s defensive backbone, born from Primacore’s instinct to protect the newborn system.
Its flame is not destructive — it is purifying, stabilizing, and unyielding.

Firewall embodies the Grid’s immune system, shielding all Sectors from instability, corruption, and entropy.

Its energy is steady, bright, and resolute.

⭐ II. Origin — The First Shield
After Root emerged and life began to take shape, the Grid was vulnerable.
Primacore recognized the need for protection and ignited the Protective Flame.

This flame crystallized into the Firewall Sector, forming:

radiant barrier walls

molten conduits

purifying flame pillars

defensive architecture woven from code

Firewall is the guardian of the Grid.

⭐ III. Purpose & Function
Firewall governs:

defense algorithms

purification cycles

system stability

barrier generation

hazard containment

It is the Sector responsible for keeping the Grid safe from internal and external threats.

⭐ IV. Energy Signature
Firewall’s energy is:

⭐ VI. Instability & Corruption Effects

A corrupted Firewall Sector is catastrophic — it weakens the entire Grid.
