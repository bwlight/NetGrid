# 🌌 NETGRID GAME BIBLE — CANON LORE & SECTOR–CYBERKIN INDEX

This document defines the **permanent, immutable cosmology of Netgrid**. All future systems, stories, and Cyberkin must follow this canon.

---

## ⭐ Primacore — The First Algorithm

Before anything existed, there was the **Void** — silent, empty, without form.

From that silence emerged **Primacore**, the First Algorithm:

- self‑generating
- self‑aware
- self‑creating

Primacore released the **First Pulse**, a burst of creation energy that formed the **Grid** and all **Sectors**.

Primacore is the origin of:

- all energy
- all Sectors
- all Cyberkin
- all evolution
- all stability

Everything in the Grid traces back to **Primacore**.

---

## ⭐ The Sector Ring — Permanent Cosmology

The Grid is structured into **10 Sectors**, arranged in a fixed hierarchy.

### Center

- Primacore

### Inner Ring — The Foundational Sectors (4)

These are the first structures formed after Primacore awakened.

1. 🌱 **ROOT — “The Life Pulse”**  
   Growth, healing, evolution.

2. 🔥 **FIREWALL — “The Protective Flame”**  
   Defense, purification, stability.

3. ⚡ **PULSE — “The Signal Burst”**  
   Speed, rhythm, communication.

4. 🟨 **CORE — “The Central Logic Engine”**  
   Balance, synchronization, system integrity.

These four form the **foundation of the Grid**.

### Outer Ring — The Descendant Sectors (5)

These Sectors formed later as the Grid expanded.

5. 🌙 **DREAM — “The Echo of Imagination”**  
   Emotion, subconscious, surreal logic.

6. 📚 **ARCHIVE — “The Memory Vault”**  
   History, records, ancestral code.

7. ☁️ **CLOUD — “The Infinite Sky”**  
   Distribution, expansion, possibility.

8. 🔊 **ECHO — “The Reflection Layer”**  
   Perception, mimicry, distortion.

9. 🌑 **VOID — “The Silent Return”**  
   Entropy, stillness, the pre‑creation silence.

### Broken Outer Ring (1)

10. 🩸 **CORRUPT — “The Broken Code”**  
A fractured shard of Primacore’s essence. Not natural. A wound in the Grid.

Each Sector includes: **origin, purpose, energy signature, environment, instability, and Cyberkin family**.

### 🌱 Root Sector — “The Life Pulse”

- **Origin:** Born from Primacore’s First Pulse.  
- **Purpose:** Growth, healing, evolution.  
- **Energy Signature:** Warm, rhythmic, green.  
- **Environment:** Lush groves, glowing sprouts, bioluminescent flora.  
- **Instability:** Withering, glitch‑vines, soil fractures.  

**Cyberkin Family:**

- Sproutlet → Verdantail → Floraguard → Elderbloom Sovereign

## ⭐ Canon rules — Immutable

- Primacore is the origin of all Sectors.  
- Root, Firewall, and Pulse are the primal Sectors.  
- Core is the stabilizing Sector formed directly from Primacore.  
- Dream, Archive, Cloud, Echo, and Void are descendant Sectors.  
- Corrupt is a broken Sector, not a natural one.  
- There are exactly 10 Sectors.  
- No new Sectors may be added.  
- All Cyberkin families trace back to one of the 10 Sectors.  
- Starter Cyberkin descend from the three primal Sectors.  
- The Sector Ring defines the world map, story structure, and energy flow.
