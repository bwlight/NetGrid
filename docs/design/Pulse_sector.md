⚡ SECTOR 03 — PULSE
“The Signal Burst”
Primal Sector — One of the First Three

⭐ I. Sector Overview
Pulse is the nervous system of the Grid — the Sector where signals flow, rhythms form, and communication becomes possible.
Born from the moment Primacore’s awakening produced the first repeating pattern, Pulse embodies speed, connection, and momentum.

Its energy is bright, electric, and alive with rhythm.

Pulse is the Sector that keeps the Grid moving.

⭐ II. Origin — The First Rhythm
After Root and Firewall stabilized the Grid’s early structure, Primacore released a second wave of creation — a repeating oscillation.

This oscillation became the First Rhythm, and from it, the Pulse Sector formed.

Pulse emerged as:

neon pathways

flowing signal streams

rhythmic data currents

waveform‑based architecture

Pulse is the beat of the Grid.

⭐ III. Purpose & Function
Pulse governs:

communication protocols

signal transmission

network synchronization

It ensures that every Sector can communicate with every other Sector.

Without Pulse, the Grid goes silent.
