⭐ I. Sector Overview
Cloud is the Grid’s distributed layer, a drifting archipelago of floating Nodes and vapor‑coded platforms.
It represents expansion, scalability, and the freedom to move without boundaries.

Cloud is light, airy, and ever‑shifting — a Sector defined by possibility.

Its energy is soft, sky‑blue, and constantly in motion.

This expansion created:

floating data clusters

vapor‑coded platforms

distributed processing Nodes

Cloud is the Sector where the Grid learned to scale.
