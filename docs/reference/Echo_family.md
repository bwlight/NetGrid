⭐ III. Family Lore
The Echo Family originated in the Resonant Hall, a vast chamber where the Grid’s ambient frequencies converge.
These creatures evolved to interpret and manipulate resonance patterns, becoming living instruments of harmonic logic.

Echo Cyberkin are known for their emotional sensitivity — they respond to tone, cadence, and even the “emotional frequency” of their bonded partner.
They often serve as communicators, signal interpreters, and illusion‑weavers.

Legends tell of an Echomancer who once stabilized a collapsing Node by generating a perfect counter‑resonance, saving an entire Sector from harmonic implosion.
