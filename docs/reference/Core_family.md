⭐ III. Family Lore
The Core Family originated in the Prime Nexus, the central hub where the Grid’s foundational logic is stored.
These creatures evolved as natural stabilizers, reinforcing system structure and preventing entropy from spreading.

Core Cyberkin are revered across the Grid for their reliability and calm presence.
They often serve as guardians of important Nodes, protectors of citizens, and mediators during Sector disputes.

Legends tell of a Coreprime who held the Nexus together during the First Collapse, preventing a chain‑reaction that could have shattered the entire Grid.
