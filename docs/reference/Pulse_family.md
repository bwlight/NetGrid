Personality Tendencies: Brave, Neutral

4. Thundragon (Final)
A majestic, serpentine dragon made of pure signal‑energy.
Thundragon is the embodiment of Pulse Sector’s rhythm — a living storm of speed and sound.

Role: Speedster / Striker / Burst Damage
Personality Tendencies: Brave, Chaotic
Element Notes: Gains immunity to paralysis and rhythm disruption

⭐ III. Family Lore
The Pulse Family originated in the Resonance Corridor, where high‑frequency data streams form natural “pulse rivers.”
These creatures evolved to navigate and harmonize with the Sector’s rhythmic energy, becoming living conduits of motion and sound.

Pulse Cyberkin are beloved across the Grid for their expressive personalities and musical movement patterns.
They often serve as signal runners, rhythm stabilizers, and emotional communicators — their bodies literally pulse with their feelings.

Legends speak of a Thundragon whose roar synchronized an entire collapsing Sector, restoring harmony long enough for the Grid to stabilize.
