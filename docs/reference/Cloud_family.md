⭐ III. Family Lore
The Cloud Family originated in the Skybound Expanse, a vast region of floating platforms, drifting islands, and atmospheric data currents.
These creatures evolved to navigate shifting airflows, adapting their forms to maintain balance and visibility.

Cloud Cyberkin are known for their gentle curiosity and serene presence.
They often act as scouts, messengers, and environmental monitors — sensing changes in weather‑code and alerting others to instability.

Legends tell of a Cloudeer who guided an entire caravan across a collapsing sky‑bridge by weaving a stable airflow path beneath their feet.
