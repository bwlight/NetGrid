⭐ III. Family Lore
The Corrupt Family originated in the Fractured Abyss, a region where broken code, failed simulations, and abandoned processes accumulate.
When instability reaches a critical threshold, corruption coalesces into living anomalies — the first Glitchip.

Corrupt Cyberkin are feared across the Grid.
They spread viral decay, override logic, and mutate unpredictably.
Yet some scholars believe corruption is not evil — merely a natural counterforce to stagnation, pushing the Grid to evolve.

Legends speak of a Ruinborn Prime that nearly tore open a Sector boundary, forcing Void and Firewall forces to unite in a desperate purge.
