⭐ III. Family Lore
The Firewall Family originated during the early stabilization of the Grid, when Primacore’s architects forged flame‑based guardians to contain the first Corrupt outbreaks.
These creatures were designed not merely to fight — but to purify, shield, and maintain order.

Firewall Cyberkin are revered as protectors across the Grid.
Their presence is often a sign of safety, and many Sectors request their aid during instability events.

Legends tell of a Blazeguard who stood alone at the edge of a collapsing Node, holding back a Corrupt surge until reinforcements arrived — a story retold in Firewall culture as “The Last Ember.”
