The Dream Family embodies imagination, emotion, and probability‑bending logic.
They are the Grid’s surreal wanderers — creatures who drift between states of thought, dream, and simulated reality.

Dream Cyberkin are:

⭐ III. Family Lore
The Dream Family originated in the Uncertainty Nexus, a region where emotional data and probability streams overlap.
These creatures evolved to navigate shifting logic, adapting to environments where reality is fluid and outcomes are never fixed.

Dream Cyberkin are beloved for their gentle, whimsical nature — but also feared for their unpredictability.
They can sense emotional states, respond to subconscious signals, and even alter probability in subtle ways.

Ancient stories tell of a Dreamora who stabilized a collapsing Dream Sector by weaving a “probability cocoon,” preserving thousands of lives in a single surreal moment.
