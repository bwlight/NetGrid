📖 II. Lore
Flarepup is a loyal young flame‑hound whose body burns with disciplined heat. Unlike the playful Embercub, Flarepup has begun to understand its role as a protector. Its flames flare brightest when defending allies, and its bark carries a resonant heat‑pulse that warns enemies to keep their distance.

Firewall citizens often train Flarepups as early‑stage guardians for important Nodes.

⚔️ III. Combat Identity
Role: Defender / Support
Strengths: Strong defensive buffs, heat‑based protection, reliable anti‑corruption tools
Weaknesses: Moderate speed, limited offensive pressure

Signature Mechanics:

Burning Resolve — boosts defense and grants temporary burn‑resistance

Heat Shield — reduces incoming physical damage

Firewall Guard — protects allies from corruption spikes

💛 V. Bonding Notes
Flarepup bonds through shared discipline and acts of bravery.
It respects players who show courage, consistency, and a willingness to stand their ground.

🩸 VI. Corruption Variant
Corrupted Flarepup becomes a snarling, unstable flame‑hound with red‑static fire patterns.
Its protective instincts invert, causing it to lash out unpredictably.
It gains Corrupt Blaze — a burn + corruption hybrid attack.
