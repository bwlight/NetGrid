📖 II. Lore
Flarelord is the Firewall Sector’s ultimate guardian — a towering sovereign of disciplined flame. Its armor burns with pure, controlled fire, and its presence radiates unwavering authority. Legends say that when a Flarelord steps onto the battlefield, corruption recoils instinctively, unable to withstand its purifying heat.

Firewall citizens regard Flarelord as a living symbol of protection, discipline, and unwavering resolve.
