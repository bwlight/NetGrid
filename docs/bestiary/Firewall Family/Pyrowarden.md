📖 II. Lore
Pyrowarden stands as one of the Firewall Sector’s elite guardians — a disciplined sentinel forged in the heart of the Emberline Forges. Its armor‑like plating glows with controlled flame, and its presence radiates unwavering vigilance. Pyrowarden is often stationed at critical Nodes, where its purifying fire keeps corruption at bay and its disciplined stance inspires nearby allies.

⚔️ III. Combat Identity
Role: Tank / Defender
Strengths: Exceptional defense, strong anti‑corruption tools, battlefield control, reliable shielding
Weaknesses: Moderate speed, limited burst damage

Signature Mechanics:

Aegis Flame — grants a massive shield and burn‑counter to attackers

Purifying Blaze — removes corruption and deals bonus damage to corrupted foes

Firewall Rampart — reduces incoming damage for the entire team

Pyrowarden is the Firewall Family’s first true “wall.”

💛 V. Bonding Notes
Pyrowarden bonds through discipline, shared purpose, and acts of protection.
It respects players who show courage, consistency, and a strong moral compass.

Once bonded, it becomes a steadfast guardian who never wavers.

🩸 VI. Corruption Variant
Corrupted Pyrowarden becomes a molten, unstable juggernaut.
Its disciplined flames twist into chaotic red‑static fire, and it gains Corrupt Aegis — a shield that damages allies and enemies alike.

Its protective instincts invert into oppressive aggression.
