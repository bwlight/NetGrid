📖 II. Lore
Embercub is a playful spark‑beast born from the Firewall Sector’s protective flame‑nodes. Its body radiates gentle warmth, and its tiny embers dance with emotion. Though small, Embercub instinctively positions itself between allies and danger, showing early signs of the Firewall Family’s legendary protective nature.

⚔️ III. Combat Identity
Role: Support / Trickster
Strengths: Fast for a baby stage, early access to heat‑based debuffs, natural resistance to corruption
Weaknesses: Low defense, fragile against heavy physical attackers

Signature Mechanics:

Heat Pulse — reduces enemy attack by applying heat‑distortion

Warm Guard — minor shield for allies

Firewall Flicker — small chance to burn attackers

💛 V. Bonding Notes
