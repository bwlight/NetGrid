📖 II. Lore
Murmlet forms when a Wispup’s drifting glow condenses into a stable dream‑shape. Its body is wrapped in soft mist that trails behind it like a half‑remembered thought. Murmlet communicates through faint murmurs — not quite words, not quite music — that soothe allies and subtly distort enemy perception. Dream Sector travelers often follow Murmlets as guides through shifting dream‑paths.

⚔️ III. Combat Identity
Role: Support / Illusion / Disruptor

Strengths:

Strong illusion tools

Accuracy disruption

Evasion boosts

High stability for a Rookie

Weaknesses:

Low physical defense

Limited direct damage

Vulnerable to Pulse burst attacks

Signature Mechanics:

Whisper Field — creates a zone that lowers enemy accuracy and softens incoming damage

Mist Veil — increases team evasion

Lucid Echo — illusion attack that may confuse or misdirect foes

Murmlet excels at soft control and battlefield distortion.

💛 V. Bonding Notes
Murmlet bonds through emotional calm, gentle interaction, and shared imagination.
It becomes deeply attached to players who show empathy and patience.

When bonded, Murmlet’s mist subtly shifts to reflect the player’s mood.

🩸 VI. Corruption Variant
Corrupted Murmlet becomes a trembling nightmare‑wisp wrapped in jagged red‑static fog.
Its murmurs turn into distorted whispers that unsettle allies.
It gains Rot Veil — a corruption mist that lowers stability and accuracy.
