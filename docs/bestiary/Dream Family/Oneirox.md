📖 II. Lore
Oneirox is the Dream Sector’s apex illusionist — a sovereign of drifting thought and living imagination. Its form is fluid, shifting between moonlit silhouettes and shimmering dream‑constructs. Oneirox doesn’t merely create illusions; it curates reality, weaving memories, emotions, and possibilities into a tapestry that bends the world around it.

Legends say Oneirox can guide lost dreamers through entire lifetimes within a single night, offering clarity, catharsis, or revelation.

Elite battlefield control

Terrain‑altering illusions

Accuracy and perception manipulation

Extremely high stability

Disruption tools that bypass physical defenses

Weaknesses:

Signature Mechanics:

Mirage Domain — reshapes the battlefield into an illusionary terrain

Dreamlock — traps an enemy in a looping illusion, delaying their action

Mind Fade — reduces enemy stability and accuracy

Oneirox doesn’t overpower foes — it makes them question what’s real.
