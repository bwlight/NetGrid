📖 II. Lore
Wispup is a gentle dream‑spirit born from the drifting imagination‑clouds of the Dream Sector. Its body glows softly, shifting color with its emotions. Though timid, Wispup is deeply empathetic — it senses the feelings of others and responds with comforting light or playful illusions. Many Dream Sector citizens keep Wispups as companions to soothe nightmares and guide young dreamers.
