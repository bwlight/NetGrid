📖 II. Lore
Reverial emerges when a Murmlet’s drifting mist condenses into a stable, elegant form. Its body flows like liquid moonlight, and its presence calms the mind while subtly bending perception. Reverial is revered in the Dream Sector as a guide of clarity — a being capable of turning nightmares into gentle visions or weaving illusions so convincing they feel like memories.

Travelers often seek Reverials when navigating the shifting dream‑paths that connect the Dream Sector’s floating isles.

⚔️ III. Combat Identity
Role: Illusion / Support / Controller

Strengths:

Powerful battlefield distortion

Accuracy manipulation

Terrain‑altering illusions

High stability for resisting corruption

Weaknesses:

Vulnerable to heavy Pulse burst damage

Signature Mechanics:

Reverie Bloom — creates a dream‑zone that lowers enemy accuracy, boosts ally evasion, and may induce “soft confusion”

Mind Veil — shields allies with illusionary barriers

Echo Mirage — illusion attack that can misdirect enemy targeting

Reverial excels at controlling the flow of battle through perception.
