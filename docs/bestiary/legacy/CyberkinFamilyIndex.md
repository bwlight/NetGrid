## 🌐 The 10 Sectors of Netgrid
Each Sector represents a distinct domain of digital reality — its own physics, logic, and elemental identity.
Cyberkin born within a Sector inherit its traits, strengths, and behavioral patterns.

## 🌱 ROOT SECTOR — Stability & Logic
Cyberkin Family: Root Family
Theme: Foundation, structure, growth, stability
Role: Defensive anchors of the Netgrid
Final Form: Rootspire

Root Cyberkin reinforce the digital world’s structural integrity, stabilizing Nodes and preventing collapse.

## ⚡ PULSE SECTOR — Energy & Signal
Cyberkin Family: Pulse Family
Theme: Electricity, rhythm, speed, signal flow
Role: High‑speed attackers and data couriers
Final Form: Voltflare

Pulse Cyberkin thrive in fast‑moving data streams and rhythmic energy patterns.

## 📚 ARCHIVE SECTOR — Memory & Pattern
Cyberkin Family: Archive Family
Theme: Logic, recursion, pattern recognition
Role: Analysts, controllers, puzzle‑solvers
Final Form: Archivorn

Archive Cyberkin manipulate patterns, loops, and recursive structures.

## 🌈 DREAM SECTOR — Probability & Imagination
Cyberkin Family: Dream Family
Theme: Probability, surreal logic, branching outcomes
Role: Illusionists and unpredictability specialists
Final Form: Reveria

Dream Cyberkin bend logic, phase through reality, and alter probability fields.

## ☁️ CLOUD SECTOR — Exploration & Adaptation
Cyberkin Family: Cloud Family
Theme: Movement, adaptability, environmental awareness
Role: Scouts, explorers, pathfinders
Final Form: Cloudeer

Cloud Cyberkin navigate shifting landscapes and multi‑Sector routes.

## 🔊 ECHO SECTOR — Memory & Illusion
Cyberkin Family: Echo Family
Theme: Reverberation, mimicry, signal echoes
Role: Illusionists and memory manipulators
Final Form: Echomancer

Echo Cyberkin distort signals, replay memories, and create phantom data.

## 🔥 FIREWALL SECTOR — Protection & Purification
Cyberkin Family: Cindrake Line
Theme: Defense, heat, purification, shielding
Role: Guardians and protectors
Final Form: Aegisflare

Firewall Cyberkin defend the Netgrid from corruption and intrusion.

## 🟡 CORE SECTOR — Prime Stability
Cyberkin Family: Core Family
Theme: Order, balance, prime logic
Role: Central guardians of the Netgrid
Final Form: Coreprime

Core Cyberkin maintain the equilibrium of the entire system.

## 🕳️ VOID SECTOR — Null & Purging
Cyberkin Family: Void Family
Theme: Nullification, deletion, entropy
Role: Purifiers and corruption erasers
Final Form: Voidreaver

Void Cyberkin purge corrupted data and stabilize collapsing Nodes.

## 🟣 CORRUPT SECTOR — Mutation & Glitch
Cyberkin Family: Corrupt Family
Theme: Glitching, mutation, entropy, instability
Role: Unstable threats and unpredictable anomalies
Final Form: Malwaregeist

Corrupt Cyberkin mutate rapidly and spread digital infection.
