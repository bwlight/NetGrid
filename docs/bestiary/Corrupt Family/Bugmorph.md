📖 II. Lore

Bugmorph forms when a Glitchlet absorbs too much corrupted data and stabilizes into a crawling, shifting creature. Its body is a patchwork of broken polygons, red‑static veins, and half‑rendered limbs that appear and disappear as it moves.

Bugmorphs scuttle through the Corrupt Sector like living malware, slipping through cracks in the terrain and leaving trails of distortion behind them. They’re unpredictable, aggressive, and constantly mutating — sometimes sprouting extra limbs, sometimes flattening into a smear of static, sometimes splitting into afterimages.

They aren’t evil. They’re simply what happens when life evolves in a place where nothing is stable.
