📖 II. Lore

Malgore is what happens when corrupted code stops flickering and starts fighting back. Its body is a constantly shifting mass of jagged polygons, red‑static tendrils, and malformed limbs that appear and vanish as it moves. Every step leaves behind distortion ripples that warp the terrain.

Unlike Bugmorph, which mutates out of instinct, Malgore mutates out of predatory intent. It hunts anything stable — not out of malice, but because stability is “food” to it. Archivists describe Malgores as “living malware with a survival instinct.”

In the Corrupt Sector, Malgores roam the deeper rifts, tearing through broken data and absorbing whatever fragments they can stabilize long enough to use.

Malgore excels at chaotic offense, destabilization, and overwhelming momentum.

💛 V. Bonding Notes

Malgore bonds through survival, persistence, and exposure.It respects players who don’t flinch, don’t panic, and don’t break under pressure.

Once bonded, its mutations become slightly more predictable — but only for the player.
