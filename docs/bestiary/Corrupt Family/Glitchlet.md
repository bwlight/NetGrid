📖 II. Lore

Glitchlet is a newborn error‑sprite created when corrupted code fragments collide and momentarily stabilize. Its body flickers between shapes — sometimes a cube, sometimes a smear of static, sometimes a half‑formed creature. No two Glitchlets look exactly alike, and even the same Glitchlet may appear different from moment to moment.

In the Corrupt Sector, Glitchlets drift through red‑static fields, nibbling on broken data and leaving behind trails of distortion. They’re unpredictable, mischievous, and strangely curious — often poking at stable Cyberkin just to watch them react.

Despite their chaotic nature, Glitchlets are not inherently malicious. They’re simply alive in a place where nothing is stable.
