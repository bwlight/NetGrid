📖 II. Lore

Chaoshive is the apex of corruption‑born life — a massive, shifting core of red‑static code surrounded by orbiting fragments, limbs, and glitch‑constructs that appear and vanish like flickering sub‑processes. It is not a single creature but a cluster, a hive of unstable routines all anchored to one central corruption‑core.

Archivists describe Chaoshive as “a living error‑system,” capable of spawning temporary glitch‑minions, rewriting its own form mid‑battle, and warping the terrain around it. Wherever it moves, the world bends — geometry distorts, textures smear, and stable Cyberkin feel their code strain under the pressure.

Chaoshive is not malicious. It is simply the natural conclusion of life evolving in a place where nothing is stable.

💛 V. Bonding Notes

Chaoshive bonds through exposure, resilience, and acceptance of chaos.It respects players who remain calm in unstable environments and adapt quickly.

Once bonded, its mutations become slightly less harmful to the player — but never predictable.
