📖 II. Lore
Beatlet is a tiny spark‑bug born from Pulse Sector rhythm‑nodes. Its body constantly vibrates with jittery energy, causing it to hop unpredictably as if following an unheard beat. Though fragile, Beatlet’s enthusiasm is infectious, and its rhythmic sparks often sync with the emotional tempo of its bonded partner.

⚔️ III. Combat Identity
Role: Trickster / Speed Support
Strengths: Extremely fast for a baby stage, strong evasion, early access to speed‑boosting abilities
Weaknesses: Very fragile, low stability, vulnerable to heavy hits

Signature Mechanics:

Beat Burst — quick multi‑hit spark attack

Static Skip — increases evasion for one turn

Pulse Hop — boosts speed for itself or an ally

Beatlet is all about tempo control.
