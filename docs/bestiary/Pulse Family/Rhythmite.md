📖 II. Lore
Rhythmite evolves from Beatlet once its internal spark stabilizes into a steady tempo. Its body emits visible pulse‑waves that ripple across its plating, syncing with ambient sounds and emotional energy. Rhythmite is beloved in the Pulse Sector for its upbeat nature — it often dances, hops, or bounces in time with nearby rhythms, whether natural or artificial.
