📖 II. Lore
Doodlit is a tiny sprout‑creature born from the Root Sector’s living code‑gardens. Its glowing leaf‑antenna pulses softly as it observes the world with quiet curiosity. Though timid, it forms deep bonds quickly and thrives when surrounded by routine and gentle care.

⚔️ III. Combat Identity
Role: Support
Strengths: Stability, early‑game defense boosts, low corruption susceptibility
Weaknesses: Low speed, low physical defense, vulnerable to burst damage

Signature Mechanics:

Integrity Boost — raises ally defenses

Sprout Shield — small protective barrier

High evasion against corruption effects

💛 V. Bonding Notes
Doodlit bonds through gentle interaction, consistent routines, and emotional calm.
It mirrors the player’s patience and becomes one of the most loyal early companions.

🩸 VI. Corruption Variant
Corrupted Doodlit becomes a thorned, unstable sprout with erratic growth patterns.
It gains Rot Flick — a tiny corruption DOT — but loses its defensive stability.
