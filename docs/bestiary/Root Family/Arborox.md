📖 II. Lore
Arborox is the Root Sector’s ultimate guardian — a towering, ancient being whose bark‑plated body resembles a living fortress. Its roots extend deep into the land, stabilizing terrain and purifying corruption wherever it stands. Entire groves have grown around Arborox as it slumbers, its presence a blessing of protection and harmony.

⚔️ III. Combat Identity
Role: Tank / Guardian
Strengths: Immense stability, powerful shielding, battlefield‑wide protection, near‑immunity to corruption
Weaknesses: Extremely slow, vulnerable to high‑speed Pulse burst combos

Signature Mechanics:

Elder Bloom — massive team‑wide shield + regeneration

Verdant Fortress — drastically reduces incoming damage

Nature’s Bulwark — purifies corruption and stabilizes terrain

Arborox is the Root Family’s final expression of resilience.

💛 V. Bonding Notes
Arborox bonds through shared purpose and emotional steadiness.
It respects players who show patience, compassion, and a desire to protect others.

Once bonded, Arborox becomes a lifelong guardian — unwavering and immovable.

🩸 VI. Corruption Variant
Corrupted Arborox becomes a monstrous tangle of thorned roots and unstable growth.
Its bark fractures into jagged corruption plates, and it gains Rotquake — a corruption‑infused ground rupture.

It loses its stabilizing aura, becoming a walking hazard.
