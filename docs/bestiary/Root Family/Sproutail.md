📖 II. Lore
Sproutail grows from a timid sprout into a sturdy young guardian. Its leafy tail acts as both a balance tool and a protective shield, swaying rhythmically as it positions itself between allies and danger. Root citizens often rely on Sproutail to watch over young seedlings and maintain harmony in communal groves.

⚔️ III. Combat Identity

Weaknesses: Low speed, limited offensive options

Anchor Field — creates a protective zone reducing incoming damage

💛 V. Bonding Notes
