📖 II. Lore
Verdantusk is a towering Root guardian, its tusks etched with ancient growth‑patterns that pulse with stabilizing energy. It moves slowly but purposefully, acting as a living bulwark against corruption and chaos. Root Sector elders often call Verdantusk “the walking grove,” a symbol of resilience and harmony.

⚔️ III. Combat Identity
Role: Tank / Support
Strengths: Massive stability, strong shielding, powerful regeneration, excellent corruption resistance
Weaknesses: Very slow, vulnerable to high‑speed Pulse attackers

Signature Mechanics:

Guardian Bloom — grants a large shield and minor regen to all allies

Verdant Wall — reduces incoming damage for the entire team

Major Regrowth — strong self‑healing over time

💛 V. Bonding Notes
Verdantusk bonds through shared purpose and acts of protection.
It respects players who show patience, leadership, and a desire to protect others.

🩸 VI. Corruption Variant
Corrupted Verdantusk becomes a twisted mass of thorned bark and unstable growth.
Its tusks fracture into jagged corruption spikes, and it gains Rot Charge — a corruption‑infused rush attack.
