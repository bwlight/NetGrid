📖 II. Lore
Noteglyph forms when a Mindlet condenses its drifting thought‑lines into a stable glyph‑core. Its body resembles a floating tablet of light, etched with shifting runes that rearrange themselves as it processes information. These glyphs aren’t decorative — they’re functional memory‑structures, each representing a concept, emotion, or fragment of archived data.

Noteglyphs drift through the Archive Sector’s Memory Wells, reading echoes of past events and learning to interpret emotional signatures. They’re gentle but deeply analytical, often pausing mid‑air to “rewrite” their own glyphs as they refine their understanding of the world.

Role: Analyzer / Controller / Support

Weaknesses:

Signature Mechanics:

Cognitive Lock — restricts enemy actions for a turn

Recall Thread — restores stability to allies

Glyph Flick — a quick mental jab

Noteglyph excels at mental pressure, disruption, and battlefield information control.

💛 V. Bonding Notes
