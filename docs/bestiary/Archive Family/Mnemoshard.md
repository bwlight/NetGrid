📖 II. Lore
Mnemoshard forms when a Noteglyph compresses its shifting glyph‑lines into a dense crystalline core. Its body resembles a floating shard of translucent memory‑crystal, etched with deep runes that glow whenever it processes information.

These creatures act as anchors within the Archive Sector — stabilizing memory streams, reinforcing data structures, and cutting through corrupted illusions with razor‑sharp clarity. Mnemoshards are revered by Archivists for their ability to “freeze” a moment of thought and examine it from every angle.

Their presence is calming, analytical, and quietly powerful.
