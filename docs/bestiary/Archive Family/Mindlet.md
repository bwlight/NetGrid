📖 II. Lore
Mindlet is a newborn thought‑sprite formed from drifting fragments of memory‑code. Its body resembles a soft, floating glyph with faintly glowing lines that shift as it processes new information. Mindlets are naturally curious, quietly observing everything around them and storing impressions in their internal memory lattice.

In the Archive Sector, Mindlets drift through Memory Wells and Data Repositories, absorbing ambient thoughts and learning to interpret emotional signals. They are gentle, contemplative creatures — the first spark of cognition in the Archive evolutionary line.
