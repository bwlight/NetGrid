📖 II. Lore

Archivalon is the pinnacle of Archive‑aligned Cyberkin — a towering, elegant construct formed from layered memory‑architecture and stabilized thought‑matter. Its body resembles a floating monument of interlocking glyph‑panels, each one representing a preserved idea, emotion, or historical echo.

Where Mnemoshard is a shard of memory, Archivalon is the library.

It drifts through the Archive Sector like a silent curator, maintaining the integrity of Memory Wells, repairing corrupted thought‑streams, and enforcing cognitive order. Archivists revere Archivalon as a guardian of truth — a being capable of perceiving the emotional resonance behind events, not just the data itself.

Its presence is calm, immense, and quietly overwhelming.
