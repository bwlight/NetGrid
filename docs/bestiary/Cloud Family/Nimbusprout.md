📖 II. Lore
Nimbusprout is a newborn cloud‑sprite formed from condensed sky‑code and drifting atmospheric data. Its body is soft and airy, constantly shifting shape as tiny static sparks dance across its surface. Nimbusprout is playful but timid — it drifts toward calm winds and hides inside thicker cloudbanks when startled.

In the Cloud Sector, Nimbusprouts are often seen floating near Weather Nodes, absorbing ambient pressure changes and learning to ride the currents.
