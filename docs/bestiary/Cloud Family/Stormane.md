📖 II. Lore
Stormane forms when a Flurmling condenses its swirling vapor into a sleek, aerodynamic body. Its mane is a living stormfront — a flowing crest of charged vapor that crackles with static and shifts color with atmospheric pressure.

Stormanes race across the Cloud Sector’s sky‑bridges, leaving spiraling wind trails behind them. They’re known for their boldness, darting into storm cells to absorb raw sky‑energy and emerging with even sharper control over wind and pressure.

Travelers often describe Stormanes as “the wind given personality.”
