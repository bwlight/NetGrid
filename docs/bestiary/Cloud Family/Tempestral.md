📖 II. Lore
Tempestral is the Cloud Sector’s apex sky‑sovereign — a living stormfront shaped into a majestic creature of wind, lightning, and atmospheric code. Its mane becomes a roaring tempest, its body a swirling mass of condensed cloudstuff reinforced by sky‑pressure.

Legends say Tempestral can reshape entire weather systems with a single cry. When it spreads its wings, the air pressure drops, winds spiral outward, and the sky itself seems to bow. Travelers describe encounters with Tempestral as “standing before the will of the sky.”

It is not merely fast — it is the wind.

⚔️ III. Combat Identity
Role: Controller / Disruptor / Evasion

Strengths:

One of the fastest non‑legendary Cyberkin

Extreme evasion and mobility

Battlefield control through pressure and wind

Accuracy disruption and stagger tools

Weather manipulation at full power

Weaknesses:

Moderate physical defense

Relies on tempo and momentum

Vulnerable to Root grounding and Core stability bursts

Signature Mechanics:

Tempest Crown — summons a storm halo that boosts speed, increases evasion, and may stagger all enemies

Pressure Burst — destabilizes enemy footing

Cyclone Step — rapid repositioning with dodge bonuses

Static Drizzle — accuracy debuff

Tempestral dominates by controlling the entire flow of battle — speed, accuracy, movement, and pressure.
