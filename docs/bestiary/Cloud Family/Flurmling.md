📖 II. Lore
Flurmling forms when a Nimbusprout absorbs enough sky‑code to stabilize into a more defined shape. Its body resembles a swirling puff of cloudstuff with streaks of static‑charged vapor curling around it. Flurmlings are known for their playful mischief — darting between cloudbanks, tugging at wind currents, and creating tiny weather anomalies just to amuse themselves.

Travelers in the Cloud Sector often see Flurmlings spiraling around Weather Nodes, learning to manipulate pressure and airflow with growing confidence.
