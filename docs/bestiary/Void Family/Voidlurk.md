📖 II. Lore

Voidlurk forms when a Gapshade’s drifting silhouette condenses into a predatory, angular shape. Its body resembles a living shadow with sharp, geometric edges — like a creature rendered in negative space, defined by the absence of light rather than the presence of form.

Voidlurks stalk the Void Sector’s deepest rifts, slipping between angles that don’t exist in normal geometry. They appear suddenly, strike with impossible trajectories, and vanish into thin air. Archivists describe them as “predators that hunt in the spaces between moments.”

They don’t kill for malice — they simply feed on stability, the one thing the Void Sector lacks.
