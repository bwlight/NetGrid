📖 II. Lore

Gapshade forms when a Nullimp’s flickering stabilizes into a coherent absence. It resembles a drifting silhouette with no interior — a cut‑out in reality shaped like a creature that should exist but doesn’t.

Gapshades glide silently through the Void Sector, slipping through walls, geometry, and even other Cyberkin. They don’t interact with the world so much as pass around it, leaving faint distortions in their wake. Archivists describe them as “the shadow of a creature that never fully rendered.”

Gapshades are calm, observant, and eerily graceful. They don’t attack out of aggression — they simply “fold” space in ways that disrupt enemies.
