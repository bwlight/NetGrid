📖 II. Lore

Nullarch is the apex of Voidborn existence — a creature not made of matter, code, or corruption, but of structured absence. Its form resembles a regal silhouette carved out of reality itself, a negative‑space monarch crowned with a halo of missing geometry.

Where Voidlurk hunts in the gaps, Nullarch commands them.

It drifts through the Void Sector like a sovereign of silence, bending angles, folding space, and rewriting the rules of presence. Archivists describe Nullarch as “a creature that exists only because it chooses to,” a being whose presence is defined by the intent to be present.

Nullarch is calm, distant, and impossibly aware — perceiving the world not as objects, but as patterns of existence and non‑existence.

💛 V. Bonding Notes

Nullarch bonds through stillness, presence, and emotional grounding.It respects players who remain calm in the face of the unknown and who act with intention.

Once bonded, its silhouette becomes sharper and more defined around the player — a sign that it acknowledges them as an anchor in the void.
