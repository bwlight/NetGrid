📖 II. Lore
Coreprime is the Core Family’s perfected form — a fully realized system‑guardian forged from refined, harmonized code. Its body is a seamless lattice of luminous plates, each etched with stabilizing algorithms that resonate in perfect sync. Unlike Primacore, the mythic origin, Coreprime is a modern pinnacle: the highest evolution a Core‑aligned Cyberkin can naturally achieve.

Its presence radiates structured calm, suppressing corruption and reinforcing system integrity across entire Nodes. When Coreprime roars, the Grid listens — its voice is a stabilizing command that restores order to chaotic data streams.
