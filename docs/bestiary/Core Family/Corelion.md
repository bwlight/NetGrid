📖 II. Lore
Corelion forms when a Bytecub’s interlocking plates refine into a sleek, noble structure. Its body resembles a lion sculpted from luminous system‑code, each plate shifting with perfect mathematical precision. Corelion is revered throughout the Core Sector as a frontline guardian — a stabilizing force that protects vital Nodes and suppresses corruption before it can spread.

Its roar resonates like a system‑wide diagnostic ping, restoring order wherever it echoes.

⚔️ III. Combat Identity

Weaknesses:

System Wall — creates a powerful barrier that reduces incoming damage

Kernel Purge — removes debuffs and deals bonus damage to corrupted foes

Data Maul — a heavy physical strike with a chance to interrupt

Corelion is the Core Family’s first true “system guardian.”
