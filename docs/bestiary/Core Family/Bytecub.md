📖 II. Lore
Bytecub forms when a Bitling’s geometric fragments fuse into a more cohesive structure. Its body resembles a small, blocky creature made of interlocking data‑plates, each glowing with clean system‑light. Bytecub is known for its protective instincts — it often positions itself between allies and danger, reinforcing system code to prevent corruption from spreading.

Core engineers rely on Bytecubs to maintain stability in high‑traffic data corridors, where micro‑errors can cascade into major faults.

⚔️ III. Combat Identity
Role: Stabilizer / Support / Defender

Strengths:

High stability for a Rookie

Strong defensive tools

Cleansing and patching abilities

Excellent anti‑corruption utility

Weaknesses:

Low offensive pressure

Slow movement

Vulnerable to Pulse burst damage

Signature Mechanics:

System Guard — boosts ally stability and grants a temporary shield

Code Barrier — reduces incoming damage for the team

Kernel Repair — heals and removes debuffs

Data Bash — simple physical strike with a chance to stagger

Bytecub is the Core Family’s first true “protector.”

💛 V. Bonding Notes
Bytecub bonds through routine, reliability, and shared problem‑solving.

Once bonded, Bytecub’s plates shift into patterns that reflect the player’s emotional “signal.”

🩸 VI. Corruption Variant
Corrupted Bytecub becomes a jagged, desynced error‑construct.
Its plates misalign, rotating unpredictably with red‑static flickers.
It gains Rot Guard — a corrupted shield that harms allies and enemies alike.
