📖 II. Lore
Bitling is a newborn data‑sprite formed from the Core Sector’s purest system code. Its body is a cluster of glowing geometric fragments that shift and rotate with mathematical precision. Though small, Bitling plays a vital role in maintaining system harmony — patching micro‑errors, smoothing corrupted edges, and stabilizing local code.

Core engineers often describe Bitlings as “living debugging tools,” though they’re far more curious and expressive than their appearance suggests.

⚔️ III. Combat Identity

Bitling is all about early‑game stability and error correction.
