## Quest Title
When the Echo Calls

## Quest Code
BOND.04

## Quest Type
Bond Quest

## Summary
Your Cyberkin hears a corrupted echo calling to it — a remnant of a past self. You must help it confront the echo and reject the corruption.

## Objectives
- Track the echo  
- Confront the Echo‑Kin  
- Anchor your Cyberkin’s identity  
- Break the echo’s influence  

## Emotional Beat
Your Cyberkin fears becoming corrupted like its past self. You help it see that it is stronger now.

## Rewards
- Bond Level +1  
- Echo Ward ability
