## **Quest Title**
Echo of the Heart

## **Quest Code**
BOND.02

## **Quest Type**
- Bond Quest
- Emotional Resonance

## **Narrative Purpose**
This quest deepens the emotional bond between the player and their Cyberkin after the confrontation with the Prime Heart. The Cyberkin struggles with the visions of its past selves and needs the player’s support to stabilize its identity.

## **Quest Summary**
After leaving the Chamber of Cycles, the Cyberkin becomes withdrawn and unstable. It keeps hearing echoes of its past selves — some corrupted, some lost. The player must take it to a quiet region of the Convergence Expanse, help it confront its fractured memories, and anchor its identity through resonance.

## **Objectives**
- Travel to the Quiet Expanse  
- Listen to your Cyberkin’s fragmented memories  
- Confront the Echo‑Kin (a manifestation of its past selves)  
- Anchor your Cyberkin’s identity  
- Strengthen your emotional bond  

## **Cyberkin Emotional Beat**
Your Cyberkin trembles as it sees its past selves.  
It fears becoming corrupted like them.  
You reassure it, grounding it with resonance.  
It emerges stronger, more confident, and more connected to you.

## **Major Story Revelations**
- Cyberkin retain echoes of past cycles.  
- Your Cyberkin once fell to corruption in a previous cycle.  
- Your bond is what keeps it stable in this cycle.

## **Rewards**
- Bond Level +1  
- Heartlink ability  
- Emotional Stability passive
