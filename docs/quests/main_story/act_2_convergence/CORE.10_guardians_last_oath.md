## **Quest Title**
The Guardian’s Last Oath

## **Quest Code**
CORE.10

## **Sector**
Sector Two — Heartbound Gate

## **Quest Type**
- Main Story
- Core Arc
- Act II Finale (Part 1)

## **Narrative Purpose**
This quest concludes the Core Sector storyline for Act II. The Guardian prepares to make their final stand, revealing the truth about the Prime Heart and the cycles. The player must defend the Guardian long enough for them to open the Heartbound Gate — the final passage to the Prime Heart’s domain.

## **Quest Summary**
The player reaches the Heartbound Gate, where the Guardian stands ready to fulfill their final oath. The corruption launches a massive assault to prevent the Gate from opening. The player must defend the Guardian, stabilize the Gate’s resonance, and defeat the Heartbound Colossus. The Guardian reveals the truth: the Prime Heart is awakening, and only the player can confront it.

## **Objectives**
- Reach the Heartbound Gate  
- Defend the Guardian from corruption waves  
- Stabilize the Gate’s resonance  
- Defeat the Heartbound Colossus  
- Witness the Guardian’s final oath  
- Enter the Heartbound Passage  

## **Sector State**
The Gate is under siege.  
Corruption tendrils lash out violently.  
Heartbound Colossus roars with ancient power.  
The Guardian is exhausted but resolute.

## **Key Characters**
- **Guardian:** Sector Two Guardian  
- **Allies:** Your starter Cyberkin  
- **Enemies:** Corruption waves, Heartbound Colossus

## **Cyberkin Emotional Beat**
Your Cyberkin becomes fiercely protective of the Guardian.  
It pushes itself beyond its limits during the siege.  
During the Colossus battle, it channels both Somnia’s Blessing and Guardian’s Valor.

## **Gameplay Mechanics Introduced or Highlighted**
- Multi-wave Guardian defense  
- Gate resonance stabilization  
- Colossus multi-phase battle  
- Guardian-assisted resonance channeling  

## **Major Story Revelations**
- The Guardian reveals the Prime Heart’s true purpose: to reset the cycle.  
- The player’s past self once stood here and failed to protect the Guardian.  
- The Prime Heart is awakening faster than expected.  
- The Guardian entrusts the player with their final oath: “Break the cycle.”

## **Memory / Lore Fragment Awarded**
- **Fragment Name:** The Guardian’s Oath  
- **Fragment Meaning:** A memory of the Guardian kneeling before the player’s past self, swearing loyalty.

## **Rewards**
- Resonance Level 16  
- Oathkeeper ability  
- Access to the Prime Heart’s domain  

## **Quest Completion State**
The Gate opens.  
The Guardian collapses, their strength spent.  
The path to the Prime Heart lies ahead.
