## **Quest Title**
Storm of the Forgotten

## **Quest Code**
CONVERGENCE.04

## **Sector**
Convergence Expanse — Stormheart

## **Quest Type**
- Main Story
- Convergence Arc
- Act II Midpoint Boss

## **Narrative Purpose**
This quest is the Act II midpoint climax. The corruption storm reaches full strength, revealing the Forgotten — remnants of Codeborn from past cycles who were consumed by the Prime Heart. The player must confront the Stormheart Entity, a fusion of Echoes, memories, and corrupted resonance.

## **Quest Summary**
The corruption storm erupts into a full-scale maelstrom. The Forgotten emerge — twisted remnants of Codeborn who failed in past cycles. The player must navigate the storm, protect their Cyberkin from resonance collapse, and confront the Stormheart Entity. Defeating it reveals the truth: the Prime Heart is awakening, and the cycle is nearing its breaking point.

## **Objectives**
- Enter the Stormheart  
- Navigate the corruption maelstrom  
- Confront the Forgotten  
- Protect your Cyberkin from resonance collapse  
- Defeat the Stormheart Entity  
- Witness the Prime Heart vision  

## **Sector State**
The Stormheart is chaotic and deadly.  
Lightning made of corrupted resonance strikes constantly.  
The Forgotten wander in agony.  
The Stormheart Entity pulses with ancient power.

## **Key Characters**
- **Guardian Echoes:** Somnia and the Sector Guardians  
- **Allies:** Your starter Cyberkin  
- **Enemies:** The Forgotten, Stormheart Entity

## **Cyberkin Emotional Beat**
Your Cyberkin is nearly overwhelmed by the Forgotten’s despair.  
It clings to you, trembling, but refuses to give up.  
During the Stormheart battle, it channels Somnia’s blessing and the Guardian’s valor.

## **Gameplay Mechanics Introduced or Highlighted**
- Storm navigation  
- Forgotten behavior  
- Resonance collapse management  
- Stormheart Entity multi-phase battle  

## **Major Story Revelations**
- The Forgotten are Codeborn from past cycles.  
- The Prime Heart consumes Codeborn who fail.  
- The player’s past self is among the Forgotten.  
- The Prime Heart is awakening — and it remembers the player.

## **Memory / Lore Fragment Awarded**
- **Fragment Name:** The Prime Heart Awakens  
- **Fragment Meaning:** A vision of the Prime Heart opening its eye for the first time in this cycle.

## **Rewards**
- Resonance Level 14  
- Stormbreaker ability  
- Access to the Convergence Depths  

## **Quest Completion State**
The storm subsides.  
The Forgotten vanish into the void.  
A path opens toward the Prime Heart’s domain.
