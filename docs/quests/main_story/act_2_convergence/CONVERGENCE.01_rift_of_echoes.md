## Quest Summary
After Somnia’s sacrifice and the Guardian’s warning, the player is dragged into the Convergence Rift — a chaotic realm where Dream and Core overlap. The player must navigate shifting terrain, confront hybrid Echo‑Beasts, and face the Rift‑Warden, a corruption entity that spans both layers. The Rift reveals a memory of the player’s past cycle failing at this exact moment. Stabilizing the Rift opens the path deeper into Act II.
