After stabilizing the Echoing Path, the player emerges into the Convergence Expanse — a massive hybrid region where Dream fractals and Core terrain blend seamlessly. The player must explore the Expanse, confront hybrid corruption entities, and locate the Resonance Pillars that stabilize the region.
