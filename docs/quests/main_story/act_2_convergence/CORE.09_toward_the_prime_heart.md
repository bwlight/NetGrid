## **Quest Title**
Toward the Prime Heart

## **Quest Code**
CORE.09

## **Sector**
Sector Two — Heartbound Passage

## **Quest Type**
- Main Story
- Core Arc

## **Narrative Purpose**
This quest pushes the Core storyline toward its climax. The Guardian reveals the location of the Heartbound Passage — a hidden route that leads directly toward the Prime Heart’s influence. The player must stabilize the passage and confront corruption entities that guard the way.

## **Quest Summary**
After breaking the siege in the Inner Frontier, the Guardian leads the player to the Heartbound Passage, a hidden corridor that connects Sector Two to the Prime Heart’s domain. The corruption has fortified the passage with Heartbound Sentinels. The player must clear the passage, stabilize its resonance, and prepare for the final convergence.

## **Objectives**
- Follow the Guardian to the Heartbound Passage  
- Stabilize corrupted resonance anchors  
- Defeat Heartbound Sentinels  
- Protect your Cyberkin from resonance drain  
- Unlock the Heartbound Gate  
- Prepare for the final convergence  

## **Sector State**
The Passage is heavily corrupted.  
Heartbound Sentinels guard every turn.  
Resonance anchors flicker violently.  
The Guardian struggles to maintain stability.

## **Key Characters**
- **Guardian:** Sector Two Guardian  
- **Allies:** Your starter Cyberkin  
- **Enemies:** Heartbound Sentinels, Corruption Wards

## **Cyberkin Emotional Beat**
Your Cyberkin becomes exhausted by the Heartbound resonance.  
It pushes itself to keep up with the Guardian.  
During the Sentinel battles, it channels Guardian’s Valor and Somnia’s Blessing.

## **Gameplay Mechanics Introduced or Highlighted**
- Heartbound resonance hazards  
- Sentinel multi-phase battles  
- Anchor stabilization  
- Guardian-assisted resonance channeling  

## **Major Story Revelations**
- The Heartbound Passage was created by the first Codeborn.  
- The Prime Heart’s influence reaches into every Sector.  
- The Guardian reveals that the player’s past self once walked this path.  
- The corruption is preparing for the final convergence.

## **Memory / Lore Fragment Awarded**
- **Fragment Name:** The Path Once Walked  
- **Fragment Meaning:** A memory of the player’s past self entering the Heartbound Passage with Somnia at their side.

## **Rewards**
- Resonance Level 15  
- Heartbound Pulse ability  
- Access to the Prime Heart’s outer domain  

## **Quest Completion State**
The Passage stabilizes.  
The Guardian prepares for the final confrontation.  
A path opens toward the Prime Heart’s domain.

## **Follow-Up Quests Unlocked**
- CONVERGENCE.06 — The Prime Heart’s Call
