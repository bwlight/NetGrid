## **Quest Title**
The Prime Heart’s Call

## **Quest Code**
CONVERGENCE.06

## **Sector**
Convergence Depths — Heart’s Threshold

## **Quest Type**
- Main Story
- Convergence Arc
- Act II Finale (Part 1)

## **Narrative Purpose**
This quest brings the player to the threshold of the Prime Heart’s domain. The Prime Heart begins speaking directly to the player, revealing fragments of truth about the cycles and the nature of corruption. This quest sets up the Act II finale and the emotional stakes for the confrontation ahead.

## **Quest Summary**
After reaching the Heart’s Veil, the player steps into the Heart’s Threshold — a region where the Prime Heart’s influence is absolute. The Prime Heart calls to the player by name, showing visions of past cycles and offering cryptic warnings. The player must navigate the shifting terrain, confront Heartbound Echoes, and stabilize the final resonance anchors before entering the Prime Heart’s chamber.

## **Objectives**
- Enter the Heart’s Threshold  
- Survive the Prime Heart’s resonance waves  
- Confront Heartbound Echoes  
- Protect your Cyberkin from resonance collapse  
- Stabilize the final resonance anchors  
- Witness the Prime Heart’s vision  

## **Sector State**
The Threshold pulses like a living organism.  
Dream and Core elements fuse into organic structures.  
Heartbound Echoes drift like shadows of past selves.  
The Prime Heart’s voice echoes through the void.

## **Key Characters**
- **Guardian Echoes:** Somnia, Sector Guardians  
- **Allies:** Your starter Cyberkin  
- **Enemies:** Heartbound Echoes, Resonance Wraiths

## **Cyberkin Emotional Beat**
Your Cyberkin is overwhelmed by the Prime Heart’s voice.  
It clings to you, trembling, but refuses to turn back.  
During the Heartbound Echo encounters, it channels Somnia’s Lullaby to stay grounded.

## **Gameplay Mechanics Introduced or Highlighted**
- Resonance wave dodging  
- Heartbound Echo behavior  
- Anchor stabilization under collapse  
- Vision-triggered memory sequences  

## **Major Story Revelations**
- The Prime Heart is sentient and ancient.  
- The cycles began long before the first Codeborn.  
- The player’s past self reached this point and made a choice that doomed the cycle.  
- The Prime Heart remembers every failure — and every Codeborn consumed.

## **Memory / Lore Fragment Awarded**
- **Fragment Name:** The Heart’s Truth  
- **Fragment Meaning:** A vision of the Prime Heart showing the player their past self kneeling before it, whispering: “I’m sorry.”

## **Rewards**
- Resonance Level 16  
- Heartcall ability  
- Access to the Prime Heart’s chamber  

## **Quest Completion State**
The Threshold stabilizes.  
The Prime Heart’s eye opens fully.  
A path forms toward the Act II finale.
