## **Quest Title**
Sector Two: The Shattered Frontier

## **Quest Code**
CORE.07

## **Sector**
Sector Two — Shattered Frontier

## **Quest Type**
- Main Story
- Core Arc

## **Narrative Purpose**
This quest shows the consequences of the convergence event on the physical world. Sector Two begins to fracture as Dream energy bleeds into the Core layer. The player must stabilize the region and locate the Guardian before the corruption consumes the Frontier entirely.

## **Quest Summary**
The player arrives in the Shattered Frontier, where Dream fractals have begun appearing in the physical world. The corruption reacts violently to the convergence, creating unstable zones and hybrid enemies. The player must stabilize the Frontier, rescue trapped Cyberkin, and locate the Guardian, who is struggling to contain the collapse.

## **Objectives**
- Survey the Shattered Frontier  
- Stabilize corrupted Frontier Nodes  
- Rescue trapped Cyberkin  
- Defeat hybrid corruption packs  
- Locate the Guardian  
- Prepare for the next convergence wave  

## **Sector State**
The Frontier is fractured and unstable.  
Dream fractals float through the air.  
Hybrid corruption entities roam freely.  
The Guardian’s resonance is weak and scattered.

## **Key Characters**
- **Guardian:** Sector Two Guardian  
- **Allies:** Your starter Cyberkin  
- **Enemies:** Hybrid corruption packs, Frontier Devourers

## **Cyberkin Emotional Beat**
Your Cyberkin becomes distressed seeing Dream fragments in the physical world.  
It senses Somnia’s fading presence and becomes protective.  
During the Frontier stabilization, it channels Dream energy instinctively.

## **Gameplay Mechanics Introduced or Highlighted**
- Hybrid environmental hazards  
- Frontier Node stabilization  
- Cyberkin rescue mechanics  
- Hybrid corruption battles  

## **Major Story Revelations**
- Dream energy is leaking into the Core layer.  
- The corruption is accelerating its spread.  
- The Guardian fears the entire Sector may collapse.  
- The convergence event is affecting all Sectors simultaneously.

## **Memory / Lore Fragment Awarded**
- **Fragment Name:** The Shattered Cycle  
- **Fragment Meaning:** A memory of a past cycle where Sector Two collapsed during convergence.

## **Rewards**
- Resonance Level 12  
- Frontier Stabilizer ability  
- Access to the Frontier’s inner region  

## **Quest Completion State**
The Frontier stabilizes temporarily.  
The Guardian warns of an incoming convergence wave.  
A path opens toward the next major region.
