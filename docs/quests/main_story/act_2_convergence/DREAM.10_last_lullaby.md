## Quest Summary
After preserving the Last Dream, the player follows a faint resonance trail to Somnia’s Heart — the final fragment of the Dream Sector. The region is nearly gone, held together only by Somnia’s fading will. The player must gather the last pieces of her essence, confront the Echo‑Requiem (a corruption entity feeding on her final memories), and witness Somnia’s final message before the Dream Sector dissolves forever.
