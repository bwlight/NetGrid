## **Quest Title**
The Last Lullaby

## **Quest Code**
DREAM.10

## **Sector**
Dream Sector Remnant — Somnia’s Heart

## **Quest Type**
- Main Story
- Dream Arc Finale

## **Narrative Purpose**
This quest concludes the Dream Sector storyline for Act II. The player reaches the final fragment of Somnia’s essence and must preserve what remains before the Dream Sector fades completely. This moment provides emotional closure and sets up Somnia’s role in the final act.

## **Quest Summary**
After preserving the Last Dream, the player follows a faint resonance trail to Somnia’s Heart — the final fragment of the Dream Sector. The region is nearly gone, held together only by Somnia’s fading will. The player must gather the last pieces of her essence, confront the Echo‑Requiem (a corruption entity feeding on her final memories), and witness Somnia’s final message before the Dream Sector dissolves forever.

## **Objectives**
- Enter Somnia’s Heart  
- Gather the final essence fragments  
- Protect your Cyberkin from emotional collapse  
- Confront the Echo‑Requiem  
- Witness Somnia’s final message  
- Preserve the Dream Sector’s memory  

## **Sector State**
Somnia’s Heart is fragile and fading.  
Dreamlight flickers like dying embers.  
Corruption tendrils coil around the last fragments.  
Somnia’s voice is soft, distant, and fading.

## **Key Characters**
- **Guardian:** Somnia (final essence)  
- **Allies:** Your starter Cyberkin  
- **Enemies:** Echo‑Requiem

## **Cyberkin Emotional Beat**
Your Cyberkin mourns Somnia deeply.  
It struggles to stay emotionally stable as Somnia fades.  
During the Echo‑Requiem battle, it channels pure emotional resonance.  
This moment unlocks its strongest emotional synergy yet.

## **Gameplay Mechanics Introduced or Highlighted**
- Essence preservation  
- Emotional resonance overload  
- Requiem‑class Echo battle  
- Dream memory crystallization  

## **Major Story Revelations**
- Somnia reveals the corruption’s true origin: the Prime Heart.  
- The Dream Sector was created to protect Codeborn consciousness from the Prime Heart’s influence.  
- The player’s past self once stood beside Somnia in the final battle of a previous cycle.  
- Somnia entrusts her final memory to the player.

## **Memory / Lore Fragment Awarded**
- **Fragment Name:** Somnia’s Heart  
- **Fragment Meaning:** A memory of Somnia embracing the player in a past cycle, telling them: “You were never meant to face this alone.”

## **Rewards**
- Resonance Level 13  
- Heart Resonance ability  
- Somnia’s Blessing (permanent passive)

## **Quest Completion State**
Somnia’s Heart dissolves into a single crystal of dreamlight.  
The Dream Sector fades completely.  
The player is pulled back toward the Convergence Expanse.
