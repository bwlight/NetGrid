## **Quest Title**
Depths of the Prime Heart

## **Quest Code**
CONVERGENCE.05

## **Sector**
Convergence Depths — The Heart’s Veil

## **Quest Type**
- Main Story
- Convergence Arc

## **Narrative Purpose**
This quest brings the player into the deepest region of the Convergence, where the Prime Heart’s influence is strongest. The player begins to understand the true nature of the cycles and the corruption’s purpose. This quest sets up the Act II finale and the first direct confrontation with the Prime Heart’s will.

## **Quest Summary**
After defeating the Stormheart Entity, the player descends into the Convergence Depths, a region where Dream and Core energy collapse into a single, pulsing reality. The Prime Heart’s whispers echo through the void, calling the player by name. The player must navigate the Depths, confront Heart‑Shades of past Codeborn, and reach the Heart’s Veil — the final barrier before the Prime Heart’s domain.

## **Objectives**
- Descend into the Convergence Depths  
- Navigate the Heart’s Veil  
- Confront Heart‑Shades  
- Protect your Cyberkin from resonance corruption  
- Reach the Prime Heart’s threshold  
- Witness the Heart’s first awakening  

## **Sector State**
The Depths pulse with living resonance.  
Dream and Core elements fuse into organic structures.  
Heart‑Shades drift like lost souls.  
The Prime Heart’s whispers distort reality.

## **Key Characters**
- **Guardian Echoes:** Somnia, Sector Guardians  
- **Allies:** Your starter Cyberkin  
- **Enemies:** Heart‑Shades, Resonance Phantoms

## **Cyberkin Emotional Beat**
Your Cyberkin becomes frightened by the Prime Heart’s voice.  
It clings to you, trembling, but refuses to turn back.  
During the Heart‑Shade encounters, it channels Somnia’s Lullaby to stay grounded.

## **Gameplay Mechanics Introduced or Highlighted**
- Heart‑Shade behavior  
- Resonance corruption management  
- Depth navigation  
- Veil stabilization  

## **Major Story Revelations**
- The Prime Heart is the source of all cycles.  
- The corruption is not mindless — it is the Prime Heart’s will.  
- The player’s past self reached this point before and failed.  
- The Prime Heart remembers every cycle and every Codeborn.

## **Memory / Lore Fragment Awarded**
- **Fragment Name:** The First Heartfall  
- **Fragment Meaning:** A memory of the player’s past self collapsing before the Prime Heart in a previous cycle.

## **Rewards**
- Resonance Level 15  
- Heartveil ability  
- Access to the Prime Heart’s inner chamber  

## **Quest Completion State**
The Heart’s Veil stabilizes.  
The Prime Heart’s eye opens for the first time.  
A path forms toward the Act II finale.
