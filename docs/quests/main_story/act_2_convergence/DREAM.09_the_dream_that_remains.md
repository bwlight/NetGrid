## **Quest Title**
The Dream That Remains

## **Quest Code**
DREAM.09

## **Sector**
Dream Sector Remnant — The Last Dream

## **Quest Type**
- Main Story
- Dream Arc

## **Narrative Purpose**
This quest explores the remnants of the Dream Sector after its collapse. Only fragments remain, drifting through the Convergence Expanse. The player must gather what’s left of Somnia’s essence and confront an Echo that has taken her form.

## **Quest Summary**
The player enters the Last Dream, a floating remnant of the Dream Sector. Somnia’s essence flickers weakly, scattered across the region. The player must gather her fragments, confront the Echo‑Somnia — a corruption entity mimicking her — and preserve what remains of the Dream Sector before it fades forever.

## **Objectives**
- Enter the Last Dream  
- Gather Somnia’s scattered essence  
- Protect your Cyberkin from emotional overload  
- Confront Echo‑Somnia  
- Preserve the Dream Remnant  
- Unlock the Dream’s final message  

## **Sector State**
The Last Dream is fragile and fading.  
Dream fragments drift through the void.  
Corruption tendrils attempt to consume the remnants.  
Echo‑Somnia’s presence distorts the region.

## **Key Characters**
- **Guardian:** Somnia (fragmented essence)  
- **Allies:** Your starter Cyberkin  
- **Enemies:** Echo‑Somnia, corruption tendrils

## **Cyberkin Emotional Beat**
Your Cyberkin mourns Somnia’s fading presence.  
It becomes emotionally unstable during the Echo‑Somnia encounter.  
You must anchor it with resonance, deepening your bond.  
This moment unlocks a powerful emotional synergy.

## **Gameplay Mechanics Introduced or Highlighted**
- Essence gathering  
- Emotional resonance anchoring  
- Echo‑Somnia battle  
- Dream preservation mechanics  

## **Major Story Revelations**
- Somnia’s final message reveals the corruption’s origin.  
- The Dream Sector cannot be restored — only remembered.  
- The corruption is tied to the first Codeborn.  
- The player’s past self played a role in the Dream Sector’s fall.

## **Memory / Lore Fragment Awarded**
- **Fragment Name:** Somnia’s Last Memory  
- **Fragment Meaning:** A vision of Somnia speaking to the player in a past cycle, warning them of the Prime Heart.

## **Rewards**
- Resonance Level 12  
- Dream Remnant ability  
- Access to the Convergence Expanse’s inner region  

## **Quest Completion State**
The Last Dream stabilizes as a memory.  
Somnia’s essence becomes a guiding presence.  
The player is pulled back toward the Convergence Expanse.

## **Follow-Up Quests Unlocked**
