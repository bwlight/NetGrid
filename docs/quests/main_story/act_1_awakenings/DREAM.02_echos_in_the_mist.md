## Quest Title

## Sector
Dream Sector — Mistway Corridor

## Quest Summary
After stabilizing in the Dream Sector, the player follows Somnia’s guidance into the Mistway Corridor, a fog‑shrouded path where memories and corruption overlap. Strange Echoes flicker in and out of existence, mimicking past Codeborn voices. The player must navigate the corridor, protect their Cyberkin from emotional overload, and confront a stronger Echo that tries to latch onto their resonance. Completing the quest unlocks the Dream Sector’s first stable travel node and reveals a fragment of a past Codeborn’s final moments.

## Narrative Purpose
This quest deepens the player’s connection to the Dream Sector and reveals that the corruption is not just a glitch — it has memory, intention, and patterns. The player learns how Corruption Echoes behave, why they appear in dreams first, and how resonance can be used to track or repel them. This quest transitions the tone from “tutorial” to “mystery.”
