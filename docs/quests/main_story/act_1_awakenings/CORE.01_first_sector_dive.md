## Quest Summary
The player dives into the first Sector through the stabilized Threshold Gate. The environment is partially corrupted, with flickering terrain and unstable nodes. The Sector Guardian is missing or weakened. The player must explore the area, stabilize the first real-world node, and confront a corrupted creature that blocks the path deeper into the Sector.

## Narrative Purpose
This quest transitions the player from the Dream Sector into the physical world of NetGrid. It introduces the first real Sector, its Guardian, its hazards, and the corruption’s physical manifestations. The player learns how resonance interacts with the environment and how corruption affects living Cyberkin.
