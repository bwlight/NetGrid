## **Quest Title**
First Sector Dive

## **Quest Code**
CORE.01

## **Sector**
Sector 1 (starting region)

## **Quest Type**
- Main Story

## **Narrative Purpose**
This quest transitions the player from the Dream Sector into the physical world of NetGrid. It introduces the first real Sector, its Guardian, its hazards, and the corruption’s physical manifestations. The player learns how resonance interacts with the environment and how corruption affects living Cyberkin.

## **Quest Summary**
The player dives into the first Sector through the stabilized Threshold Gate. The environment is partially corrupted, with flickering terrain and unstable nodes. The Sector Guardian is missing or weakened. The player must explore the area, stabilize the first real-world node, and confront a corrupted creature that blocks the path deeper into the Sector.

## **Objectives**
- Enter the first Sector  
- Survey the corrupted terrain  
- Locate the missing or weakened Guardian  
- Stabilize the first Sector Node  
- Defeat the Corrupted Beast  
- Unlock the Sector’s main path  

## **Sector State**
The Sector is partially corrupted.  
Environmental hazards pulse with unstable energy.  
Wild Cyberkin behave erratically.  
Nodes flicker between active and dormant states.

## **Key Characters**
- **Guardian:** Sector Guardian (varies by Sector)  
- **Allies:** Your starter Cyberkin  
- **Enemies:** Corrupted Beast (first real-world corruption fight)

## **Cyberkin Emotional Beat**
Your Cyberkin experiences the physical world for the first time.  
It reacts strongly to corrupted wildlife, sensing their pain.  
During the Corrupted Beast battle, it shows courage and empathy.

## **Gameplay Mechanics Introduced or Highlighted**
- Sector exploration  
- Environmental hazards  
- Node stabilization  
- Real-world corruption battles  
- Guardian interactions  

## **Major Story Revelations**
- Corruption affects Cyberkin physically and emotionally.  
- The Guardian is weakened or missing due to corruption.  
- The Sector’s instability is spreading outward.  
- The player’s resonance can purify corruption temporarily.

## **Memory / Lore Fragment Awarded**
- **Fragment Name:** Guardian’s Plea  
- **Fragment Meaning:** A fragmented message from the Guardian begging for help before being overwhelmed.

## **Rewards**
- Sector Map unlocked  
- Resonance Level 4  
- First real-world ability upgrade  
- Access to deeper Sector regions  

## **Quest Completion State**
The first Sector Node stabilizes.  
The Guardian’s location or condition becomes the next objective.  
The corruption recedes slightly but remains a threat.

## **Follow-Up Quests Unlocked**
- CORE.02 — Guardian in the Dark  
- DREAM.04 — Somnia’s Warning (optional return to Dream Sector)
