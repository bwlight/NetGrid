## Quest Summary
The Dreamline Core’s temporary stabilization fails, triggering a catastrophic breach. Somnia summons the player to her Sanctuary, now nearly consumed by corruption. The player must defend Somnia from waves of Echoes, stabilize collapsing Dream Nodes, and confront the Echo‑Harbinger — a corruption entity that speaks with the player’s voice from a past cycle. Somnia sacrifices her remaining strength to open a path to the convergence point.
