## **Quest Title**
Guardian in the Dark

## **Quest Code**
CORE.02

## **Sector**
Sector 1 — Deep Corruption Zone

## **Quest Type**
- Main Story

## **Narrative Purpose**
This quest reveals the fate of the Sector Guardian and shows the player how deeply the corruption has infiltrated the physical world. The player must track the Guardian’s resonance trail, confront corrupted wildlife, and discover that the Guardian has been partially consumed by the corruption.

## **Quest Summary**
After stabilizing the first Sector Node, the player follows a faint resonance trail deeper into the Sector. The environment becomes darker and more unstable. Corrupted wildlife attacks in coordinated patterns, suggesting intelligence behind the corruption. The player eventually finds the Guardian, weakened and partially corrupted, unable to maintain control of the Sector.

## **Objectives**
- Follow the Guardian’s resonance trail  
- Navigate the Deep Corruption Zone  
- Defeat corrupted wildlife packs  
- Locate the Guardian  
- Purify the Guardian’s resonance  
- Stabilize the Deep Node  

## **Sector State**
The Deep Corruption Zone is heavily distorted.  
Terrain flickers between corrupted and normal states.  
Wild Cyberkin are aggressive and unpredictable.  
The Guardian’s resonance pulses weakly from the depths.

## **Key Characters**
- **Guardian:** Sector Guardian (weakened and partially corrupted)  
- **Allies:** Your starter Cyberkin  
- **Enemies:** Corrupted Wildlife Packs, Corrupted Guardian Fragment  

## **Cyberkin Emotional Beat**
Your Cyberkin senses the Guardian’s pain and becomes emotionally overwhelmed.  
It hesitates during the first encounter with corrupted wildlife.  
During the Guardian confrontation, it shows empathy and determination, helping purify the Guardian’s resonance.

## **Gameplay Mechanics Introduced or Highlighted**
- Tracking resonance trails  
- Corrupted wildlife pack behavior  
- Guardian purification  
- Deep Node stabilization  
- Environmental corruption hazards  

## **Major Story Revelations**
- The corruption is coordinated, not random.  
- The Guardian was attacked by a powerful corruption entity.  
- The corruption is spreading faster than expected.  
- The Guardian recognizes the player from a past cycle.

## **Memory / Lore Fragment Awarded**
- **Fragment Name:** Guardian’s Memory  
- **Fragment Meaning:** A vision of the Guardian fighting a massive corruption entity before falling.

## **Rewards**
- Resonance Level 5  
- Purification Pulse ability  
- Deep Node unlocked  
- Guardian’s trust  

## **Quest Completion State**
The Guardian is stabilized but weakened.  
The Sector becomes partially safe again.  
A new path opens deeper into the Sector.

## **Follow-Up Quests Unlocked**
- CORE.03 — The Heart of Corruption  
- DREAM.05 — The Echo That Knows Your Name
