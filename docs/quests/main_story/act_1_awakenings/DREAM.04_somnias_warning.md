## **Quest Title**
Somnia’s Warning

## **Quest Code**
DREAM.04

## **Sector**
Dream Sector — Somnia’s Sanctuary

## **Quest Type**
- Main Story

## **Narrative Purpose**
This quest reveals the Dream Sector’s weakening state and Somnia’s growing fear that the corruption is evolving faster than expected. The player learns that the Dream Sector is no longer a safe refuge and that their presence is accelerating the cycle. Somnia gives the player a cryptic warning about a “memory that does not belong to you.”

## **Quest Summary**
After stabilizing the Threshold Gate, the player returns to Somnia’s Sanctuary. Somnia is visibly strained, surrounded by flickering dream fractals. She explains that the corruption is beginning to mimic resonance patterns, something it has never done before. The player must help Somnia cleanse the Sanctuary, confront a new type of Echo, and receive a final warning before being sent deeper into the real Sectors.

## **Objectives**
- Return to Somnia’s Sanctuary  
- Assist Somnia in cleansing the dream fractals  
- Investigate the resonance anomaly  
- Defeat the Adaptive Echo  
- Receive Somnia’s warning  
- Prepare for deeper Sector exploration  

## **Sector State**
Somnia’s Sanctuary is unstable.  
Dream fractals pulse with corrupted resonance.  
Echoes whisper fragments of memories that feel familiar but wrong.  
Somnia’s form flickers, showing signs of strain.

## **Key Characters**
- **Guardian:** Somnia  
- **Allies:** Your starter Cyberkin  
- **Enemies:** Adaptive Echo (first Echo that reacts to player resonance)

## **Cyberkin Emotional Beat**
Your Cyberkin becomes distressed by the Adaptive Echo mimicking its emotional patterns.  
It clings closer to you, showing fear and confusion.  
During the battle, it pushes through fear to protect you, strengthening your bond.

## **Gameplay Mechanics Introduced or Highlighted**
- Cleansing dream fractals  
- Adaptive Echo behavior  
- Resonance pattern recognition  
- Emotional defense mechanics  

## **Major Story Revelations**
- The corruption is learning from the player.  
- Echoes are beginning to mimic resonance signatures.  
- Somnia reveals that the player’s arrival disrupted the cycle.  
- A memory fragment hints that the player has been here before.

## **Memory / Lore Fragment Awarded**
- **Fragment Name:** The Forgotten Self  
- **Fragment Meaning:** A distorted memory of someone with the player’s silhouette standing beside Somnia in a past cycle.

## **Rewards**
- Resonance Level 5  
- Emotional Shield ability  
- Sanctuary cleansing complete  
- Access to deeper Dream Sector paths  

## **Quest Completion State**
Somnia stabilizes the Sanctuary temporarily.  
She warns the player that the corruption is “seeking them specifically.”  
The Dream Sector becomes more dangerous moving forward.
