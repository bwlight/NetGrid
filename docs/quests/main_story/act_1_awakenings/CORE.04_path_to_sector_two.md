## **Quest Title**
The Path to Sector Two

## **Quest Code**
CORE.04

## **Sector**
Sector 1 — Guardian’s Refuge → Sector Two Gate

## **Quest Type**
- Main Story

## **Narrative Purpose**
This quest transitions the player from the first Sector into the next major region of NetGrid. The Guardian reveals that the corruption is spreading across Sectors and that the player must travel to Sector Two to stop the next Heart from awakening. The player learns about inter‑Sector travel, Guardian‑level resonance, and the growing threat of coordinated corruption.

## **Quest Summary**
After purifying the Corruption Heart, the Guardian leads the player to the Sector Two Gate. The path is filled with corruption remnants and unstable terrain. The player must clear corruption clusters, stabilize the Gate’s resonance, and confront a lingering corruption entity attempting to block the path. Once the Gate is stabilized, the Guardian opens the way to Sector Two.

## **Objectives**
- Meet the Guardian at the Refuge  
- Travel to the Sector Two Gate  
- Clear corruption clusters  
- Stabilize the Gate’s resonance  
- Defeat the Gate‑Warden corruption entity  
- Activate the Sector Two pathway  

## **Sector State**
The path to Sector Two is unstable.  
Corruption remnants pulse with lingering energy.  
Terrain shifts unpredictably.  
The Gate flickers between active and dormant states.

## **Key Characters**
- **Guardian:** Sector Guardian  
- **Allies:** Your starter Cyberkin  
- **Enemies:** Gate‑Warden (lingering corruption entity)

## **Cyberkin Emotional Beat**
Your Cyberkin feels the Guardian’s gratitude and responds with pride.  
It becomes more confident navigating corrupted terrain.  
During the Gate‑Warden battle, it shows courage and resilience.

## **Gameplay Mechanics Introduced or Highlighted**
- Inter‑Sector travel mechanics  
- Gate resonance stabilization  
- Corruption cluster clearing  
- Guardian‑assisted resonance channeling  

## **Major Story Revelations**
- The corruption is spreading across multiple Sectors.  
- Each Sector has a Heart that must be purified.  
- The Guardian hints that the corruption is being directed by a central intelligence.  
- Sector Two holds clues about the corruption’s origin.

## **Memory / Lore Fragment Awarded**
- **Fragment Name:** The Second Path  
- **Fragment Meaning:** A memory of a past Codeborn entering Sector Two and encountering a massive corruption surge.

## **Rewards**
- Resonance Level 7  
- Gate Stabilizer ability  
- Access to Sector Two  

## **Quest Completion State**
The Sector Two Gate stabilizes.  
The Guardian sends the player forward with a warning.  
The path to the next major region opens.

## **Follow-Up Quests Unlocked**
