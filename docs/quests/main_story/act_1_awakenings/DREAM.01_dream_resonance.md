## **Quest Title**
Dream Resonance

## **Quest Code**
DREAM.01

## **Sector**
Dream Sector (Entry Layer)

## **Quest Type**
- Main Story

## **Narrative Purpose**
Introduce the Dream Sector, establish the emotional bond with the true starter Cyberkin, and reveal the first hints of the corruption cycle’s deeper meaning. This quest grounds the player after the chaos of the System Breach.

## **Quest Summary**
Introduce the Dream Sector, establish the emotional bond with the true starter Cyberkin, and reveal the first hints of the corruption cycle’s deeper meaning. This quest grounds the player after the chaos of the System Breach.

## **Objectives**
- Regain consciousness in the Dream Sector
- Meet your true starter Cyberkin
- Learn resonance basics
- Follow the resonance trail
- Confront the harmless Corruption Echo
- Stabilize the Dream Node
 

## **Sector State**
The Dream Sector is calm but unstable at the edges.
Soft distortions ripple through the environment.
Dream Nodes flicker between clarity and static.
A faint hum of resonance guides the player forward. 

## **Key Characters**
- **Guardian:** Somnia (Dream Sector Guardian)
- **Allies:** Your true starter Cyberkin
- **Enemies:** Harmless Corruption Echo (tutorial enemy)   

## **Cyberkin Emotional Beat**
Your Cyberkin is scared but drawn to you.
It resonates with your presence, forming the first emotional bond.
During the Echo encounter, it shows bravery despite fear.

## **Gameplay Mechanics Introduced or Highlighted**
- Resonance sensing
- Bond strength
- Emotional feedback
- Light exploration
- First real battle (non-simulation)

## **Major Story Revelations**
- The Dream Sector stabilizes Codeborn consciousness
- The corruption cycle is older than the player
- The player’s arrival disrupted the cycle
- The Cyberkin bond is more than companionship — it’s survival

## **Memory / Lore Fragment Awarded**
- **Fragment Name:** Dreaming Core
- **Fragment Meaning:** A vision of past cycles, showing silhouettes of previous Codeborn who failed.

## **Rewards**
- Resonance Level 1
- First ability unlock
- Dream Sector travel node activated

## **Quest Completion State**
The Dream Sector stabilizes.
Your Cyberkin fully bonds with you.
Somnia acknowledges your arrival and warns of the coming corruption surge.

## **Follow-Up Quests Unlocked**
