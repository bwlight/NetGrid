## Quest Summary
After stabilizing the Gate, the player enters Sector Two. The environment is heavily corrupted, with terrain shifting unpredictably and wildlife behaving erratically. The player must explore the Arrival Zone, locate the Sector Two Guardian, and stabilize the first node. Along the way, they encounter a new type of corruption entity that reacts to the player’s resonance in unpredictable ways.
