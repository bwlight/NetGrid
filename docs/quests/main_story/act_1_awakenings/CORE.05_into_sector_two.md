## **Quest Title**
Into Sector Two

## **Quest Code**
CORE.05

## **Sector**
Sector Two — Arrival Zone

## **Quest Type**
- Main Story

## **Narrative Purpose**
This quest introduces Sector Two, a region with stronger corruption, deeper lore, and more complex environmental hazards. The player learns that Sector Two holds clues about the corruption’s origin and that the Heart in this Sector is awakening faster than expected.

## **Quest Summary**
After stabilizing the Gate, the player enters Sector Two. The environment is heavily corrupted, with terrain shifting unpredictably and wildlife behaving erratically. The player must explore the Arrival Zone, locate the Sector Two Guardian, and stabilize the first node. Along the way, they encounter a new type of corruption entity that reacts to the player’s resonance in unpredictable ways.

## **Objectives**
- Enter Sector Two  
- Survey the Arrival Zone  
- Locate the Sector Two Guardian  
- Stabilize the first Sector Two Node  
- Defeat the Resonance‑Leech  
- Unlock the Sector’s main path  

## **Sector State**
Sector Two is heavily corrupted.  
Terrain shifts between stable and unstable states.  
Wild Cyberkin show signs of deep emotional distress.  
The Guardian’s resonance is faint and scattered.

## **Key Characters**
- **Guardian:** Sector Two Guardian (weakened, hiding)  
- **Allies:** Your starter Cyberkin  
- **Enemies:** Resonance‑Leech (corruption entity that drains resonance)

## **Cyberkin Emotional Beat**
Your Cyberkin becomes exhausted as the Resonance‑Leech drains its energy.  
It struggles to maintain emotional stability.  
You must use resonance to restore its strength.  
During the battle, it unlocks a new emotional synergy.

## **Gameplay Mechanics Introduced or Highlighted**
- Sector Two environmental hazards  
- Resonance drain mechanics  
- Node stabilization under pressure  
- Guardian tracking  

## **Major Story Revelations**
- Sector Two’s corruption is more advanced and coordinated.  
- The Resonance‑Leech reacts specifically to the player’s resonance.  
- The Guardian is hiding from a powerful corruption entity.  
- Sector Two holds clues about the corruption’s origin.

## **Memory / Lore Fragment Awarded**
- **Fragment Name:** The Hidden Guardian  
- **Fragment Meaning:** A memory of the Sector Two Guardian fleeing from a massive corruption surge.

## **Rewards**
- Resonance Level 8  
- Resonance Shield ability  
- Sector Two Arrival Node unlocked  

## **Quest Completion State**
The Arrival Zone stabilizes.  
The Guardian’s location becomes the next objective.  
The corruption grows more aggressive.

## **Follow-Up Quests Unlocked**
- CORE.06 — The Guardian’s Trail  
- DREAM.08 — Somnia’s Last Stand
