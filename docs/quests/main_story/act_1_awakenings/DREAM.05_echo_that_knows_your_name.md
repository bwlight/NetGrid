## **Quest Title**
The Echo That Knows Your Name

## **Quest Code**
DREAM.05

## **Sector**
Dream Sector — Whispering Vale

## **Quest Type**
- Main Story

## **Narrative Purpose**
This quest escalates the Dream Sector threat by introducing an Echo that directly targets the player, calling them by name and mimicking their voice. It confirms Somnia’s warning that the corruption is evolving and that the player’s resonance signature is being hunted. The player learns that Echoes can now breach deeper dream layers and that their past cycles may be bleeding through.

## **Quest Summary**
After Somnia’s warning, the player follows a resonance disturbance into the Whispering Vale, a dreamscape filled with drifting memory fragments. An Echo begins whispering the player’s name, mimicking their voice and emotional tone. The player must navigate the Vale, confront the Echo, and resist its attempts to overwrite their resonance. Defeating it reveals a memory fragment that suggests the player has lived through multiple cycles.

## **Objectives**
- Enter the Whispering Vale  
- Follow the resonance disturbance  
- Locate the source of the whispers  
- Resist the Echo’s resonance overwrite  
- Defeat the Name‑Echo  
- Recover the memory fragment  

## **Sector State**
The Whispering Vale is unstable and filled with drifting memory shards.  
Echoes whisper in familiar voices.  
The environment reacts to the player’s emotional state.  
The Name‑Echo’s presence distorts the Vale’s geometry.

## **Key Characters**
- **Guardian:** Somnia  
- **Allies:** Your starter Cyberkin  
- **Enemies:** Name‑Echo (an Echo that mimics the player)

## **Cyberkin Emotional Beat**
Your Cyberkin becomes distressed hearing the Echo mimic your voice.  
It tries to shield you emotionally, showing deepening loyalty.  
During the battle, it resonates with you to break the Echo’s influence.

## **Gameplay Mechanics Introduced or Highlighted**
- Resonance overwrite resistance  
- Emotional shielding  
- Memory shard navigation  
- Echo mimicry escalation  

## **Major Story Revelations**
- The corruption can now mimic the player’s voice and emotional patterns.  
- The Echo references events the player has no memory of.  
- A past version of the player may have confronted this Echo before.  
- Somnia fears the corruption is targeting the player specifically.

## **Memory / Lore Fragment Awarded**
- **Fragment Name:** The Cycle Before  
- **Fragment Meaning:** A blurred vision of the player facing the same Echo in a previous cycle, suggesting a repeating loop.

## **Rewards**
- Resonance Level 6  
- Emotional Anchor ability  
- Whispering Vale travel node unlocked  

## **Quest Completion State**
The Whispering Vale stabilizes.  
Somnia becomes more protective and more fearful.  
The Dream Sector prepares for a deeper corruption breach.
