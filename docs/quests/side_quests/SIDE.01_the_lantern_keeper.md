## Summary
A lone Cyberkin tends a field of resonance lanterns that once guided travelers. The lanterns are fading, and the Cyberkin fears being forgotten. You must help restore the lanterns and learn the story of the Keeper who once tended them.

## Emotional Beat
The Cyberkin admits it stayed behind because it was waiting for its Keeper to return — a Keeper who never will.
