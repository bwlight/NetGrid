## Quest Title
Wings of the Fallen

## Quest Code
SIDE.06

## Sector
Sector Six — Skyforge Isles

## Quest Type
Side Quest

## Summary
A winged Cyberkin has lost its ability to fly after a corruption storm shattered its wings. You must help forge new wings using Skyforge Alloy.

## Objectives
- Collect Skyforge Alloy  
- Defeat airborne corruption  
- Reforge the Cyberkin’s wings  
- Witness its first flight  

## Emotional Beat
The Cyberkin cries with joy as it takes to the sky again.

## Rewards
- Skyforge Wings cosmetic  
- Air Step passive
