## Quest Title
Flame of the Forgotten

## Quest Code
SIDE.05

## Sector
Sector Five — Ember Warrens

## Quest Type
Side Quest

## Summary
A young Ember Cyberkin struggles to keep its flame alive. It believes it is too weak to survive. You must help it rekindle its flame by gathering ember shards.

## Objectives
- Gather ember shards  
- Defeat heat‑drained corruption  
- Rekindle the Cyberkin’s flame  
- Escort it to the Ember Shrine  

## Emotional Beat
The Cyberkin glows brightly and says, “Thank you… for believing in me.”

## Rewards
- Ember Spark cosmetic  
- Fireheart passive
