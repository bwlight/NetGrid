## **Quest Title**
Last Echo of Somnia

## **Quest Code**
DREAM.EP01

## **Sector**
Dream Remnant — The Quiet Place

## **Quest Type**
- Side Quest
- Emotional Epilogue

## **Narrative Purpose**
This quest provides emotional closure to the Dream arc without interrupting the main Act II escalation. It allows the player to revisit the remnants of Somnia’s presence, reflect on the collapse of the Dream Sector, and strengthen their bond with their Cyberkin through shared grief and remembrance.

## **Quest Summary**
After the events of Somnia’s Heart, a faint resonance calls to the player from a small, untouched fragment of the Dream Sector. This place, known as the Quiet Place, is a peaceful pocket of dreamlight that survived the collapse. The player must follow the resonance, gather lingering dream petals, and confront a gentle Echo that carries Somnia’s final lullaby. Completing the quest preserves Somnia’s memory and deepens the emotional bond with the Cyberkin.

## **Objectives**
- Follow the faint dream resonance  
- Enter the Quiet Place  
- Gather dream petals  
- Listen to the Echo’s lullaby  
- Share a moment of remembrance with your Cyberkin  
- Preserve Somnia’s final echo  

## **Sector State**
The Quiet Place is serene and untouched.  
Soft dreamlight drifts like petals in the air.  
Corruption cannot reach this place.  
Somnia’s presence lingers like a warm memory.

## **Key Characters**
- **Guardian:** Somnia (memory echo only)  
- **Allies:** Your starter Cyberkin  
- **Enemies:** None (peaceful quest)

## **Cyberkin Emotional Beat**
Your Cyberkin becomes quiet and reflective.  
It senses Somnia’s presence and presses close to you.  
When the lullaby plays, it trembles softly, remembering her warmth.  
You and your Cyberkin share a moment of grief and gratitude.

## **Gameplay Mechanics Introduced or Highlighted**
- Memory listening  
- Dream petal gathering  
- Emotional resonance bonding  
- Non-combat narrative interaction  

## **Major Story Revelations**
- Somnia left behind a final lullaby meant only for the player.  
- The Quiet Place exists because Somnia protected it with her last strength.  
- The lullaby hints that Somnia believed the player could break the cycle.  
- The Dream Sector may be gone, but its memory lives on in the player and their Cyberkin.

## **Memory / Lore Fragment Awarded**
- **Fragment Name:** Somnia’s Lullaby  
- **Fragment Meaning:** A soft melody that carries Somnia’s final words:  
  *“Even in the darkest cycle, you are not alone.”*

## **Rewards**
- Resonance Level +1 (emotional bonus)  
- Lullaby Charm (passive: reduces emotional overload in future Dream-related encounters)  
- Bond Strength Increase  

## **Quest Completion State**
The Quiet Place fades into a preserved memory.  
Somnia’s final echo becomes part of the player’s heart.  
Your Cyberkin gains a deeper emotional connection to you.  
The path forward leads back to the Convergence Expanse.
