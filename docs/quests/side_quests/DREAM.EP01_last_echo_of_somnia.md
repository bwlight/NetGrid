## Quest Summary
After the events of Somnia’s Heart, a faint resonance calls to the player from a small, untouched fragment of the Dream Sector. This place, known as the Quiet Place, is a peaceful pocket of dreamlight that survived the collapse. The player must follow the resonance, gather lingering dream petals, and confront a gentle Echo that carries Somnia’s final lullaby. Completing the quest preserves Somnia’s memory and deepens the emotional bond with the Cyberkin.
