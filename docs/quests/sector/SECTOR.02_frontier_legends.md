## **Quest Title**
Frontier Legends

## **Quest Code**
SECTOR.02

## **Sector**
Sector Two — Shattered Frontier

## **Quest Type**
- Sector Quest
- Exploration & Lore

## **Narrative Purpose**
This quest reveals the history of Sector Two before the corruption. It shows how the Frontier once thrived and how the first Codeborn shaped its identity.

## **Quest Summary**
The player discovers an abandoned Frontier outpost containing old resonance logs. These logs tell the story of the Frontier’s first explorers and their battles against early corruption. The player must restore the logs, defeat lingering corruption remnants, and uncover the Frontier’s forgotten legacy.

## **Objectives**
- Explore the abandoned outpost  
- Restore resonance logs  
- Defeat corruption remnants  
- Recover the Frontier Chronicle  
- Learn the Sector’s forgotten history  

## **Major Story Revelations**
- Sector Two was once a thriving exploration hub.  
- The first Codeborn fought corruption long before the cycles began.  
- The Frontier Guardian’s lineage traces back to the first explorers.

## **Rewards**
- Frontier Chronicle (lore item)  
- Resonance Level +1  
- Frontier Explorer cosmetic
