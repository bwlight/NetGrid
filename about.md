# Netgrid

A digital‑mythic creature RPG set inside the Grid — a vast, living network shaped by elemental families, ancient systems, and evolving digital life.

## What Is Netgrid?

Netgrid is a creature‑raising RPG that blends tactical combat, emotional bonding, and exploration across a fractured digital world. Players partner with Cyberkin — sentient data‑born creatures — and uncover the mysteries of Primacore, the mythic origin system that once unified all life in the Grid.

The game is built on a fully modular, schema‑validated engine, with every creature, ability, status, and world element defined in structured JSON.

---

## The World of the Grid

The Grid is divided into ten sectors, each shaped by a different elemental family. These sectors form the backbone of Netgrid’s world:

- Verdant Root jungles  
- High‑voltage Pulse cities  
- Mist‑shrouded Cloud realms  
- Dream‑woven psychic domains  
- Shadowed Void territories  
- Superheated Firewall forges  
- Corrupt wastelands of glitch and decay  
- Archive vaults of perfect order  
- Core industrial megastructures  
- Primacore — the mythic origin  

Each sector has its own lore, creatures, hazards, and mysteries.

---

## Cyberkin

Cyberkin are the heart of Netgrid. They evolve, bond, and grow alongside the player. Each belongs to one of the ten elemental families, shaping their abilities, personality, and combat role.

Cyberkin evolve through Rookie → Champion → Final forms, with optional branching paths influenced by stability, bonding, and world interactions.

---

## Gameplay Pillars

- **Exploration** — travel between sectors, uncover secrets, restore lost systems  
- **Bonding** — build trust, unlock evolutions, shape personality  
- **Combat** — tactical, turn‑based battles with elemental synergy  
- **Growth** — evolve Cyberkin, learn abilities, master the Grid
